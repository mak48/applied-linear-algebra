## Многочлен Лагранжа
$$
f(x) = L(x) = \sum_{i=1}^n y_i
\frac{(x - x_0)(x - x_1)...(x - x_{i-1})(x - x_{i+1})...(x - x_n)}{(x_i - x_0)(x_i - x_1)...(x_i - x_{i-1})(x_i - x_{i+1})...(x_i - x_n)},
\quad y_i = f(x_i), \quad i = 0, ..., n.
$$
